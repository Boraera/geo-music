Where we come from and where we are going


Our work started in 2012 with the idea that we should somehow affiliate these genres to do something awesomely unusual. It’s also motivation to make a difference in the thinking about the dynamic of the Hungarian music culture by making a new, different mainstream all along with lots of other bands we know and support.


In 2016 we released our first EP named Szófiai Utcakő in which lyrics of our songs express social critic, moderate political sarcasm, tolerance towards each other and world peace.


Our venues in the past included Flórián tér, Muzikum Klub, Kis Á, Gödör Klub, Magyar Színház, Bori Vár and lots of other places.


Our appearances occur monthly concentrated mainly to the area near and in Budapest.
